The Joka campus advantage

IIM Calcutta's Joka campus is one of India's most iconic and beautiful B-school campuses — regularly cited by alumni as a major part of the programme's value. The SMP includes mandatory campus immersion modules at Joka. For senior professionals, the informal networking during campus visits — with a cohort of 40–60 director-level peers from varied industries — is often cited as the highest ROI component of the entire programme.

IIMC SMP — who it works best for

  • East India professionals — Kolkata, West Bengal, Odisha, Jharkhand, northeast market
  • Finance and consulting sector leaders — IIMC's strongest alumni presence is in finance and consulting
  • PSU senior executives — IIMC has historically strong relationships with government enterprises and PSUs in east India
  • 10–15 year professionals targeting VP to CXO transition — the SMP cohort peer quality is matched only by IIMA and IIMB programmes at this experience level
