IIM Kozhikode New Age Management Programme 2026 — Complete Review

IIM Kozhikode New Age Management — what is it?

The New Age Management Programme for Senior Professionals from IIM Kozhikode is a 9-month executive programme designed for senior professionals navigating the intersection of traditional management and digital disruption. IIM Kozhikode (IIMK) is NIRF #3 in Management, Triple-Crown accredited (AACSB, AMBA, EQUIS) and FT-ranked #69 globally. Fee approximately ₹2–3L + GST. The programme runs as live weekend sessions with a campus module at IIMK's scenic Calicut campus in Kerala.

Who is the New Age Management Programme designed for?

The programme targets senior professionals with 8+ years of experience who are leading teams and business units in the current digital era. It is specifically valuable for: business unit heads and senior managers who came up through traditional functional roles and want structured exposure to digital strategy and data-driven decision making, professionals in non-tech industries (FMCG, healthcare, manufacturing, real estate) who are being asked to lead digital transformation initiatives without a formal background in digital, and CXO-aspirants who want the IIMK Triple-Crown credential to strengthen their profile for the final move to C-suite.

Curriculum — what "New Age" actually means

The programme is structured around the tension between traditional management wisdom and new-age business realities. Modules cover: digital business models and platform economics, data-driven decision making (no coding — analytics interpretation for leaders), AI and GenAI for business strategy, leading digital transformation and change, people management in hybrid and distributed organisations, customer experience in the digital era, and strategic leadership in uncertain environments. The IIMK pedagogy uses case-based learning throughout — cases from Indian and global companies at different stages of digital maturity.

Campus module at IIM Kozhikode

The campus immersion at IIMK's Calicut campus is one of the programme's most valued elements. The campus sits on a hilltop with panoramic views — the physical setting and the cohort interaction during immersion consistently receive high marks from alumni. The module typically runs for 3–5 days and includes faculty sessions, group projects, alumni networking and a campus experience that is difficult to replicate in online-only formats.

Is IIM Kozhikode New Age Management worth ₹2–3L?

The IIMK Triple-Crown credential at ₹2–3L is among the most cost-efficient senior management credentials in India — NIRF #3 institution, globally accredited, at roughly half the fee of IIM Ahmedabad AGMP. For senior professionals wanting an IIM credential that is recognised both in India and internationally (EQUIS and AMBA recognition matters for professionals with global career ambitions), IIMK delivers strong value.
